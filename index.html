Computer Sciences Building

NISER, Bhubaneswar

I am a researcher interested in Machine Learning on Graphs. Currently, I am exploring the graph generation problem. I am also working on ensuring privacy in graph data.

Currently, I am a Ph.D. candidate at School of Computer Sciences at National Institute of Science Education and Research, Bhubaneswar, India, advised by Subhankar Mishra.

Prior to this, I graduated from Goa College of Engineering with a B.E. in Computer Engineering and Goa University with a M.Tech in Computer Science.
